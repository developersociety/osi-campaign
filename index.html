Become a member of OSI today

The Open Source Initiative (OSI) is a non-profit corporation with global scope formed to educate about and advocate for the benefits of open source and to build bridges among different constituencies in the open source community.

As a member-driven community the OSI offers a variety of opportunities for individuals, open source projects & foundations, public sector organizations, institutions of higher education, and businesses to join and contribute to our mission.

Individual members are individuals who are part of the open source community and who have decided to declare their support for the open source software movement.

Membership levels

Individual Members - $40/year
  • Define the direction and identify the leadership of the OSI by nominating and voting for OSI Board Directors.
  • You can run for a Director seat. Five members of the Board represent the Individual Membership, and play a vital role in the open source community.
  • Receive exclusive offers through our Affiliate Members and Corporate Sponsors. These currently include:
    1. 20% off training from Linux Foundation. The Linux Foundation is uniquely positioned to provide the high quality, vendor neutral training on a range of cutting edge technologies like Kubernetes and Blockchain.
    2. 15% off Open Source Technology Management online courses at Brandeis University
    3. 15% off IT Leadership and IT Management training, workshops, and coaching from Hallmentum
    4. 10% off at Adafruit.com, an Open-Source Hardware manufacturer.
  • Receive a “OSI Member badge” that can be added to websites, blogs, etc. to let the community know they support open source.
Basic Members - $0 (free)
  • Receive a “Virtual OSI Member badge” that can be added to websites, blogs, etc. to let the community know they support open source.
  • Voting rights are not included in the level
